3. Are there any dangers of your final product possibly misleading the audience?

The planets are sized so that they are visible and can be spread out as visible dots, but they can mislead the audience on how big the planets are. The sizes were hard to make small due to the need to be able to click on them with relative ease, and not be so large as to crowd out other data points.

The graph numbers are limited in their description, and can cause the user to struggle to understand the scope of the variables of the planets.
